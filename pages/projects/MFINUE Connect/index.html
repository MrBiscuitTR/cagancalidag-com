Description

MFINUE Connect is a private web portal for participants of the MFINUE conference — a francophone MUN held by Istanbul Saint-Joseph French High School. It serves as a hub for schedules, committee tools, documents, tally sheets, presence tracking, and a Google Drive Picker for uploads. Started by Can Bora Ciner '24, I joined as the most active developer across the 2022–2023 and 2023–2024 editions — adding features, fixing bugs, and training the team.

Technical Information

  • Language(s): TypeScript, JavaScript, CSS
  • Framework(s): React (TSX)
  • Database(s): Firebase — Cloud Firestore & Storage
  • Hosting: Firebase
Other:
  • Authentication — Firebase Email & Password, Google OAuth
  • APIs — Google Drive Picker
